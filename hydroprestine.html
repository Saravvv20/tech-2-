The Design

Our project has two machines- ‘HydroPrestine’ and ‘Collector Deck’. HydroPrestine is a submarine which remains in the ocean and does the work of waste collection. As soon as it senses any sort of garbage near its surroundings it opens its robotic arm and pick it up for collecting accumulated or large quantity of waste it will open its waste collecting chamber. Further, inside it, the sensors will segregate the waste based on Biodegradable and Non- Biodegradable. It will have a GPS tracker as it deposits the waste collected after every two weeks Besides this, HydroPrestine is also an energy conservator. It will have a turbine and produce hydro energy. Thus, it works on self-created energy. However, some power will be provided for backup purposes.

THE PROBLEM-

Marine ecosystems are highly exposed to contamination from human activities. Recently, a growing number of emerging pollutants (i.e., nano plastics and microplastics) have been reaching the pristine environments of the Arctic or the Antarctic.For years the oceans have been targeted as a dumping site of industrial waste, chemicals (including chemical weapons), wastewater, garbage, and other waste from terrestrial sources. Dumping of some substances, i.e., radionuclides, has been banned in recent years; nevertheless, they remain in the environment.

Solutions For the Problem

Our Innovation -

‘HydroPrestine’ is the problem solver of this environmental issue. Hydroprestine is equipped with high quality sensors, night vision cameras, flashlights etc. It can collect the trash from the oceans(non-biodegradable). It has large chamber in which the collected waste is stored and later deposited in the ‘Collector Deck’, which roams around the HydroPrestine.

Functioning -

It has two chambers in which the above two types of wastes are collected in and deposited in the ‘Collector Deck’, which roams around the HydroPrestine.the ‘Collector Deck’ will remain floating in the ocean, roaming around the submarine and collect waste from the HydroPrestine after a period of 14 days. It will also be fitted with a GPS tracker to sublimate the waste in any nearby garbage collecting house.

The System

Hydropristine(our ship) contains our specially designed submarines which roam around the mighty oceans to collect non bio-degradable waste . This entire collection of a ship and submarines are named "THE BATTALION" .

Features

If our submarines are seperated by Hydropristine because of ocean waves and currents , it has a gps microchip in it which helps us to locate the submarine . If in any cases the submarine loses GPS contact with the ship , it is programmed to return to the dock where it started it's noble mission .

1.Protection of Marine and aquatic life The most visible impacts of plastic debris are the ingestion, suffocation and entanglement of hundreds of marine species. Marine wildlife such as seabirds, whales, fish and turtles mistake plastic waste for prey; most then die of starvation as their stomachs become filled with plastic. Now, these problems will be solved by our project! HydroPrestine is going to collect the wastes from the oceans and seas and save the marine ecosystem!

2.Help in controlling climate change In the past few years, ocean health is getting severely affected by inappropriate waste management systems and human activities. Moreover, there is an increase emission of greenhouse gas. HydroPrestine is the problem solver, it will help in preventing climate action and save the environment.





3.Maintaining Human Health Ocean pollution also harms human beings. Microplastics, which have now entered the food chain, have been found to be carcinogenic and can affect our body’s endocrine system, causing serious disorders and disease. Toxic contaminants pose another health-hazard. They often accumulate on plastic’s surface and are then transferred to humans through the consumption of seafood.
